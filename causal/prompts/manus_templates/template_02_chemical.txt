## KEY MECHANISMS TO LOOK FOR
- Aqueous-phase Reactions: SO2 oxidation in water → Sulfate
- Heterogeneous Reactions: NH3 + HNO3 → NH4NO3
- Photochemical Reactions: NO2 + hν → O3 formation
- Secondary Aerosol Formation: SIA (SO4^2-, NO3^-, NH4+), SOA
