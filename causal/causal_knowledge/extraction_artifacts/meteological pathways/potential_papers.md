# Potential Papers for Meteorological Pathways PM2.5 Research

## Tier-1 Papers (Peer-Reviewed)

### Temperature Inversion & PM2.5
1. **Trinh et al. (2019)** - "Temperature inversion and air pollution relationship, and its effects on human health in Hanoi City, Vietnam"
   - URL: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10653-018-0190-0
   - DOI: 10.1007/s10653-018-0190-0
   - Cited by: 154
   - Focus: Temperature inversion effects on pollutants in Hanoi

2. **Xu et al. (2019)** - "Two Inversion Layers and Their Impacts on PM2.5 Concentration"
   - URL: https://journals.ametsoc.org/view/journals/apme/58/11/jamc-d-19-0008.1.xml
   - Cited by: 25
   - Focus: Dual inversion layers and PM2.5

3. **Sun et al. (2025)** - "Multilayer temperature inversion structures and their effects on PM2.5"
   - URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1352231024006733
   - Cited by: 5
   - Focus: Multi-layer inversions in North China

### PBLH & PM2.5
4. **Su et al. (2018)** - "Relationships between the planetary boundary layer height and surface pollutants"
   - URL: https://acp.copernicus.org/articles/18/15921/2018/
   - Cited by: 321
   - Focus: PBLH-PM2.5 relationship in China

5. **Tursumbayeva et al. (2022)** - "Planetary Boundary Layer and its Relationship with PM2.5 Concentrations in Almaty"
   - URL: https://aaqr.org/articles/aaqr-21-10-oa-0294
   - Cited by: 23
   - Focus: PBLH effects on PM2.5

### Cold Surge & Air Pollution
6. **Phung-Ngoc et al. (2023)** - "Intricate behavior of winter pollution in Hanoi over the 2006-2020 period"
   - URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S135223102300095X
   - Cited by: 5
   - Focus: Cold surge effects on PM2.5 in Hanoi

7. **Aman et al. (2023)** - "A Study of Urban Haze and Its Association with Cold Surge"
   - URL: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9964824/
   - Cited by: 17
   - Focus: Cold surge and haze in Southeast Asia

8. **Influence of Northeast Monsoon cold surges on air quality in Southeast Asia**
   - URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S135223101730496X
   - Focus: Cold surge effects on air quality in SEA

### Monsoon & PM2.5 in Hanoi
9. **Influence of meteorological conditions on PM2.5 during monsoon season in Hanoi**
   - URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1352231002002959
