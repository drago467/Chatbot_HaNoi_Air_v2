# Danh sách nguồn tiềm năng - Wet Scavenging PM2.5

## Tier-1 Sources (Peer-Reviewed)

### 1. Guo et al. 2016 - Washout effects
- URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0269749116303803
- Title: The washout effects of rainfall on atmospheric particulate pollution
- Cited by: 200+
- Key: Rainfall thresholds (7mm Guangzhou, 1mm Xi'an)

### 2. Sun et al. 2019 - Light vs Heavy Precipitation
- URL: https://agupubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1029/2019EA000717
- Title: Distinct Impacts of Light and Heavy Precipitation on PM2.5
- Key: Light rain (<0.5mm, <1hr) có thể tăng PM2.5

### 3. Zhao et al. 2020 - Precipitation Intensity Impact
- URL: https://www.mdpi.com/2073-4433/11/9/906
- Title: Impact of Precipitation with Different Intensity on PM2.5
- Key: Three stages: <0.6 mm/h, 0.6-4.5 mm/h, >4.5 mm/h

### 4. Xu et al. 2019 - Below-cloud wet scavenging
- URL: https://acp.copernicus.org/articles/19/15569/2019/
- Title: Multi-method determination of the below-cloud wet scavenging
- Cited by: 48

### 5. Wang et al. 2021 - Rainfall rates importance
- URL: https://acp.copernicus.org/articles/21/16797/2021/acp-21-16797-2021.pdf
- Title: What rainfall rates are most important to wet removal

### 6. Lu et al. 2019 - Below-cloud washout effect
- URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0269749119303501
- Title: To what extent can the below-cloud washout effect influence PM2.5
- Key: Rain intensity 2-60 mm/h

### 7. MDPI 2024 - Wet Scavenging Process
- URL: https://www.mdpi.com/2073-4433/15/9/1070
- Title: Development of Wet Scavenging Process of Particles in Air Quality

### 8. Hien et al. 2002 - Hanoi PM2.5 monsoon
- URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1352231002002959
- Title: Influence of meteorological conditions on PM2.5 in Hanoi

### 9. Ho Chi Minh City rainfall impact

- Title: Impacts of rainfall on PM2.5 concentrations in Ho Chi Minh City

### 10. Duhanyan & Roustan 2011 - Below-cloud scavenging review
- URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1352231011009344
